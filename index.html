About

A bit about me

I'm a computer vision engineer passionate about building systems that extract meaning from visual data. I specialize in designing, training, and deploying deep learning models for perception tasks at scale.

My journey started with classical image processing and OpenCV, and evolved into deep learning when I saw a CNN outperform hand-crafted features on every benchmark. Today I work across the full vision pipeline -- from data collection and annotation to model optimization and edge deployment.

When I'm not training models, you'll find me reading papers on arXiv, contributing to open-source CV libraries, or experimenting with novel architectures for 3D understanding and video analysis.
